Game States
-----------
200: Game intro
300: Game start
400: End level intro animation (fadeout)
401: Level intro? (gbox.soloGroup, levelIntroAnimation)
402: Next level? (gbox.playAllGroups)
500: Wait after death
501: Fade after death
600: New life intro? (gbox.soloGroup, newLifeIntroAnimation)
601: New life? (newLife)
700: Game over lose? (reset music, gameOverIntroAnimation)
800: End level intro animation
801: Game over win? (reset music, gameEndingIntroAnimation)
